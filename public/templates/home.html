About me

Only a handful of people know how I came up with the name Logicalicy. Of course, as all good things do, it came about through an "a-ha" moment, 4AM in the morning.

As a result, I have a lot of interest in... a lot of things! To whittle down that list, however, I'm most enthusiastic about tech startups, food and sports.
